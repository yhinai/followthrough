## One sentence

Followthrough is a consent-first ambient BizDev agency that turns the conversations founders already have into researched opportunities, safe follow-up drafts, CRM memory, and completion reports before the opportunity disappears.

## Short description

Founders meet prospects, partners, and tools all day, but the useful context disappears into transcripts and handwritten notes. Followthrough listens only during a visibly consented session, discards ordinary conversation before persistence, and hands business signals to a Hermes manager. Hermes dynamically chooses specialist agents for entity resolution, live research, opportunity scoring, relationship writing, policy review, CRM memory, and reporting. Every handoff is traceable. Completed work lands in the public proof surface and the founder's real Discord DM; third-party messages remain approval-gated.
